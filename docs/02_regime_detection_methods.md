# Methods for Detecting Market Regimes

Several statistical tools can detect regime switching.

---

## Hidden Markov Models

HMM assumes:

market state = hidden variable

price returns depend on that hidden state.

Example states:

State 1 -> low volatility  
State 2 -> high volatility

---

## Volatility Clustering

Financial markets exhibit clustered volatility.

Periods of high volatility tend to persist.

GARCH models capture this.

---

## Structural Breaks

Regimes can also be detected by identifying structural breaks in time series.
